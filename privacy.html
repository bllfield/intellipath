Privacy Policy

At Intellipath Solutions, we respect your privacy. We do not sell your personal information. Data collected through our platforms is used only to provide energy insights and improve your experience.

Information Collection

We collect information that is necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Contact information you provide (name, email, etc.)
  • Energy usage data (with your permission)
  • Information about how you use our services

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Analyze energy usage patterns to offer personalized recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platforms

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@intellipath-solutions.com.
