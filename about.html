Why We Exist

We're on a mission to make energy work for people — not the other way around. By combining transparency, smart technology, and a deep understanding of real-world household needs, we're building tools that simplify energy decisions and put control back where it belongs: in the hands of the customer.

Brian Littlefield, Founder & CEO

Intellipath wasn't born from a business plan — it was born from frustration. Our founder, Brian Littlefield, spent years in finance and later in solar, only to realize both industries shared the same fatal flaw: misaligned incentives. Customers were often left in the dark while companies profited off their confusion. That realization sparked a deeper mission — not to sell more energy products, but to empower smarter energy decisions.

Intellipath exists to flip the system on its head. We don't chase commissions. We chase outcomes that actually help people — outcomes that save money, reduce waste, and build long-term trust. We're not just solving energy inefficiency. We're solving informational inefficiency — with tools, insights, and recommendations designed around what's right, not what sells.

Where We're Going

We're working toward a future where energy is no longer confusing, frustrating, or out of reach — where every household understands how their home uses power, and has the tools to take control of it.

Our vision is to transform how energy is delivered, explained, and experienced. Not through gimmicks or guesswork, but through simple, powerful solutions that put people first. We're creating a world where transparency is the norm, smart choices are effortless, and energy finally works the way it should — for the homeowner.

What We Stand For

Transparency

We earn trust by showing our work. Every recommendation we make — from plan comparisons to usage breakdowns — is backed by real data, not marketing spin.

Accountability

Our success is tied to the value we deliver. We guide decisions with clear, data-based insights — and never any pressure.

Innovation

We don't accept the way things have always been done. We challenge outdated systems and build smarter, faster, more practical tools for real homes and real people.

Impact Over Hype

We're not here to chase trends. We're here to lead meaningful change — away from confusion and toward clarity, confidence, and real savings.

"This company is a course correction — not just for my career, but for the entire energy experience homeowners have endured for too long. I built Intellipath because I knew there had to be a better way. Now we're proving it."

— Brian Littlefield, Founder & CEO

Making energy smarter, cheaper, and clearer for everyone.
