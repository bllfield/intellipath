Why We Exist

We're on a mission to make energy work for people — not the other way around. By combining transparency, smart technology, and a deep understanding of real-world household needs, we're building tools that simplify energy decisions and put control back where it belongs: in the hands of the customer.

Built by a Customer Who Cracked the Code

Intellipath wasn't born from a business plan — it was born from frustration.

Founder Brian Littlefield spent years in finance, helping families plan for their future and take control of their budgets. One expense kept slipping through the cracks: electricity.

Not because people were using too much — but because they had no idea what they should be paying.

Then it got personal.

Brian spotted the same patterns in his own home — unpredictable bills, vague rates, and plans that seemed designed to confuse instead of help. Later, while working in the solar industry, he saw the same problem from the inside: a system built on complexity and misaligned incentives.

So he set out to build the tool he wished he had.

Brian Littlefield, Founder

The Problem: Energy Isn't Just a Cost — It's a Blind Spot

Most households don't track how much energy they use, when they use it, or what it truly costs them. The result?

Hundreds — sometimes thousands — in overpayments each year.

That's not just a budgeting issue.
That's a system failure — one designed to keep customers in the dark.

The Mission: Clear, Confident Energy Decisions

At Intellipath, we believe better decisions start with better data.

We use cutting-edge tools, proprietary algorithms, and AI technology to analyze real usage patterns and uncover what's really going on behind your bill.

Then we show you the best path forward — whether that means switching plans, shifting usage habits, or making simple changes to reduce your energy waste.

No sales pressure.
No guessing.
Just clear, personalized insights built around what works for you.

The Long-Term Vision

What started with one frustrated customer has grown into a smarter energy platform for households across the country.

Our goal is simple:

Empower people to take control of their energy — and stop overpaying for power they don't need.

Because when enough people get access to real energy intelligence, the system starts to work the way it should: for the customer.

Making energy smarter, cheaper, and clearer for everyone.
