Built to Fix a Broken System

The energy industry thrives on confusion — complicated plans, vague bills, and misaligned incentives. We were founded to break that cycle. Our mission is simple: give every household access to smarter, clearer, and more cost-effective energy decisions.

From AI-powered analysis to transparent plan switching, every tool we build is designed around one goal: make energy work for people, not the other way around.

Built by a Customer Who Cracked the Code

Intellipath was born from two perspectives: one as a frustrated customer, and the other as a financial advisor who had seen too many clients quietly bleeding money on their energy bills. Our founder, Brian Littlefield, began noticing it in his own home — monthly charges that didn't add up, confusing plans, and pricing that seemed to punish loyalty instead of rewarding it.

At the same time, he was helping families manage their budgets and plan for the future — and a pattern started to emerge. Energy wasn't just a cost. It was a blind spot. People weren't overpaying by a few dollars — they were overpaying by hundreds, sometimes thousands, each year. Not because they used too much, but because the system made it nearly impossible to know what they should be paying.

That's where Intellipath comes in.

We don't sell electricity. We don't pitch upgrades. We build tools that help you understand your usage, compare plans, and stop overpaying — for good. It's your energy. You should be in control. We're here to make that possible.

We're Building the Energy Ecosystem of the Future

Intellipath is more than a switching site or a savings app. It's the foundation for a complete home energy intelligence platform. We're building tools that understand your usage, optimize your options, and evolve with your needs — from your first plan comparison to full energy independence.

This is just the beginning. As the energy landscape shifts, we'll be the platform leading the way — smarter, faster, and always on the side of the homeowner.

Making energy smarter, cheaper, and clearer for everyone.
