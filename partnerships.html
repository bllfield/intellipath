Help Shape the Future of Energy Access

We partner with innovators who believe better energy solutions start with better alignment — with the customer, not the market.

At Intellipath Solutions, we believe energy should work for people — not the other way around. We're building tools that make energy simpler, smarter, and more cost-effective for households. But to truly shift the industry, we collaborate with others who share that same commitment.

We welcome partnerships with energy retailers, technology platforms, and data providers who put consumers first and are serious about creating solutions that prioritize trust, clarity, and long-term impact.

How We Collaborate

Energy Retailers

We work with providers who believe energy plans should be clear, fair, and built around real usage. If your business is aligned with transparency and long-term customer value, we'd love to explore how our platforms can support shared goals.

Technology Partners

If you're building tech that helps people better manage their energy — whether through smart devices, automation, or analytics — we offer thoughtful integration opportunities that maintain focus on the end user.

Data Collaborators

We're always looking for responsible, privacy-minded partners who can help improve how we model usage, forecast savings, or identify new ways to help consumers cut waste and reduce costs.

Platforms With Purpose

Our brands — including Intelliwatt and HitTheJackWatt — are designed to educate, empower, and reward consumers for making better energy decisions. We're not building gimmicks — we're building long-term tools to help households take control.

We only partner with organizations that share this mindset and are committed to delivering real value to consumers, not extracting it.

Learn more about our brands on the Our Brands page.
