Working at Intellipath

We're building more than a platform — we're building a team of people who care about doing meaningful work that makes life better for others. At Intellipath, talent meets purpose, and every role is connected to real impact.

At Intellipath Solutions, we're not just building products — we're building a better energy experience for millions of households. That kind of mission requires more than talent. It requires people who care deeply about doing meaningful work, solving real problems, and making everyday life easier for others.

We're driven by purpose, not pressure. We believe that great things happen when smart, humble people come together to build something that actually matters — and that culture, collaboration, and integrity should never be afterthoughts.

We're fully staffed at the moment, but we're growing fast — and when the time is right, we'll be looking for individuals who want to be part of something bigger than a job. If you're someone who brings ideas to the table, thrives in fast-moving environments, and wants to build tools that help real people save real money, we want to hear from you.

Our Team Approach

We bring together sharp minds from energy, tech, finance, and consumer advocacy — not to maintain the status quo, but to challenge it. Our team combines deep market expertise with a relentless drive to simplify how energy works for everyday people.

We're not just building software — we're building trust. Every tool we create is designed to cut through confusion, uncover savings, and put real power back in the hands of households. Because when energy makes sense, everything else gets easier.

This isn't just a platform — it's a team built to shift the balance back to the consumer.
